About Us

We Are A Team Of Experts Who Have Deep Experience In Math Educating Middle To High Schoolers About The Extensiveness Of Math!
We Believe That Every Student Should Have Access To A Free And Meaningful Education, And Hope That We Can Help Enrich A Society Of Young Problem Solvers.

We are a diverse group of teenagers whose shared love for math drives us towards a common goal: to bring the beauty of mathematics to all. Our non-profit organization offers a plethora of services ranging from homework help to math enrichment classes from pre algebra to calculus.

No matter what career you plan on, learning and enjoying mathematics is essential in order to move up the corporate ladder and work effectively.
Knowing how to problem solve in math will help open doors to solving problems effectively and dealing with them moving on, even if your career isn't STEM related.

Our Mission

It is our goal at To The Limit Maths to make the art of mathematics and problem solving approachable and enjoyable to all. Our goal is for students to both learn and have fun while problem solving.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who runs to the limit mathematics?

    To the limit maths is run by our team of students, all in high school around the Bay Area.

  • How can I be a tutor?

    Sign up on the Google Form above and we can schedule an interview!

  • Is it free to be tutored?

    Yes, we are a nonprofit, dedicated to helping YOU without the distraction of money.

  • What math levels do you teach?

    We teach pre-algebra through calculus.

  • What experience do tutors have?

    Our tutors have experience in many types of math, both in school and competitive math.

  • Do you tutor competition math?

    We have a few tutors who can teach lower level competition math such as AMC 8. We are possibly looking to extend into the AMC 10/12 and AIME.
