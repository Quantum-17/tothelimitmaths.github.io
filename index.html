About To The Limit Maths

We are a group of passionate mathematitians that enjoy doing and teaching math. We want to bring this interest outside of our schools to teach the world about math.

We believe that every student should have access to a free and meaningful education, and hope that we can help enrich a society of young problem solvers.

To the Limit Maths is run by a team of high schoolers in the Bay Area. We are completely nonprofit and teach for free. All of our tutors have experience with both school and competition math, and have taken the courses they teach before, so they have the tools it takes to help you succeed.

Our Goal

It is our goal at To The Limit Maths to make the art of mathematics and problem solving approachable and enjoyable to all. We seek to make students both learn and have fun while problem solving. Plus, it's free!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who runs To The Limit?

    To the limit maths is run by our team of students, all in high school around the Bay Area. View our team here!

  • How can I be a tutor?

    Sign up on the Google Form above and we can schedule an interview!

  • Is it free to be tutored?

    Yes, we are a nonprofit, dedicated to helping you!

  • What math levels do you teach?

    We teach pre-algebra through calculus.

  • What experience do tutors have?

    Our tutors have experience in many types of math, both in school and competitive math.

  • Do you tutor competition math?

    We have a few tutors who can teach lower level competition math such as AMC 8. We are possibly looking to extend into the AMC 10/12 and AIME.
